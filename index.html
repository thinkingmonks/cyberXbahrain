Kuwait's journey toward achieving its "New Kuwait Vision 2035" reflects a strong commitment to establishing a digitally advanced and secure ecosystem. The nation is addressing the growing demands of cybersecurity amidst its expansive digital transformation. Through the efforts of key organizations such as the Communication and Information Technology Regulatory Authority (CITRA) and the Central Agency for Information Technology (CAIT), Kuwait is making significant strides in strengthening its cybersecurity framework.

The CyberX Kuwait Summit & Awards 2025 comes at a pivotal moment in Kuwait's journey toward digital transformation under its "New Kuwait Vision 2035." With increased digitization across industries, cybersecurity has become a cornerstone of the nation’s strategic priorities. Recognizing this, the government has introduced robust initiatives, including the National Cybersecurity Strategy, and allocated a substantial $1 billion to achieve Maturity Level 5—indicating a resilient enterprise-level cybersecurity framework—over the next five years

The CyberX Kuwait Summit & Awards 2025, taking place on 27th May 2025, is a pivotal platform to unite industry leaders, policymakers, and cybersecurity experts. Participants will delve into Kuwait's cybersecurity advancements, explore innovative defense strategies, and contribute to building a resilient digital future. Join us in shaping the narrative of cybersecurity excellence in Kuwait.

KEYNOTE SPEAKER 2025
Mr. Khalid Al Hashemi
Director of National Cyber Empowerment and Excellence Affairs at the National Cyber Security Agency (NCSA)

Khalid Al-Hashemi is the Director of National Cyber Security Enablement & Excellence Affairs at National Cyber Security Agency (NCSA). He is responsible to enable national level cyber security initiatives and projects, he is responsible for national level cyber security innovation, research, development, enablement, training and awareness programs, he also provides co-ordination between NCSA and external stakeholders and creating partnerships with related local entities to enhance national cyber security.

Khalid started his career within Industrial sector in Information Technology, then moved to cyber security domain and for the last decade he was in leadership roles in national cyber security.

Key Achievements:

  • Leading cyber security planning and execution for the CLUB World CUP 2020.
  • Leading cyber security planning and execution for the World CUP 2022.
  • Leading cyber security planning and execution for Asian Cup 2024 hosted in Qatar.
